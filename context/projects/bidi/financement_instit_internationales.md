# FUNDING REQUEST  
## ROMBONE AGRO-PEDAGOGICAL PLATFORM  
Ebolowa – Cameroon

---

## 1. Project Rationale

The agricultural potential of Southern Cameroon remains under-structured, and practical training opportunities for agricultural students are limited.

ROMBONE aims to create a 2-hectare agro-pedagogical platform integrating:

- practical agricultural training,
- structured production,
- measurable social impact.

---

## 2. Objectives

- Provide real-life agricultural training environment;
- Strengthen local food systems;
- Create local employment;
- Contribute to food security for vulnerable populations.

---

## 3. Sustainability

The platform will progressively reach operational balance from year two through structured commercialization.

---

## 4. Budget Requested

15,000,000 FCFA (approx. €23,000)

---

## 5. Impact Indicators

- Number of students trained annually;
- Volume of agricultural output;
- Quantity allocated to social structures;
- Employment generated.
